Fra Ensemble

June 2023

Video & audio, post


Marco Pereira and de Fra team reached out to me looking for a way to visually express the time-encounter-synergy in-between them and their approach to contemporary ensemble performance.

It was a 4-hour one-man production process, with a period for video recording during the rehearsal process and audio recording later on, that was also mixed by me and used for the video.
