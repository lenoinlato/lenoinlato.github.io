À Mesa 2025




uma residência de

Patrícia Pinheiro

April 2025

Filmed at Celorico de Basto

Direction, Camera, editing, fx, post


"À Mesa" is an artistic investigation collective, founded by Patrícia Pinheiro.

The documentary features its first initiative, 'Rituals and Superstitions', where Patrícia, along with Inês Luzio, Teresa Costa and Ema Ferreira, explored Celorico de Basto's local traditions and how they influence their daily life.
