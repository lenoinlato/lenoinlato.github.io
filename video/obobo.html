O BOBO




entity

André Júlio Turquesa

July 2024 - september 2024

Filmed at Porto

Direction, Camera, editing, fx, post


O BOBO is André Júlio Turquesa's 4th song out of his latest album, "DISCO DE RECLAMAÇÕES".

The song takes inspiration on a schizophrenic friend of AJT, seen both as troublesome and admirable. The videoclip explores this living between these worlds, where sanity and insanity meet at midpoint.

AJT's expression was the main focus of this video, partly inspired by David Bowie's LAZARUS videoclip. Black and white footage comes back as a tool for memory and fiction, while giving focus to the dynamics and texture of the photography.

Principal photography was done in one day.
