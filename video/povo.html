POVO




entity

O Marta

February 2024 - march 2024

Filmed at Barril do Alva / Medas / Sobral Magro / Sobral Gordo / Pomares, Coimbra

Direction, Camera, editing, fx, post


POVO is O Marta's last song from his second album, "Casta Brava".

The video is an expanded take on the themes of cultural inertia and stallness expressed by the song, throught the protagonist's relationship with herself and her memories. Memories are fragile, susceptible to the strongest of emotions, walking on a tightrope between reality and fiction, and thus between necessity and burden. Probably the closest contact to reality is the things that one moves towards growing, creating roots, pouring water, taking care of... the process that makes the movement and the place make sense.

The photography and aesthetic was a big part of the development of this project. Black and white footage has always provocked this feeling with me - memories that somehow always felt so distant to the point of fiction, it felt right to potrait this feeling. Inspirations go from Yasujirō Ozu's Tokyo Story (1953) to João Ribeiro's work in João Botelhos' The Year of the Death of Ricardo Reis (2020).

Principal photography was done in three days, comprising 5 different between Arganil and Serra do Açor.
