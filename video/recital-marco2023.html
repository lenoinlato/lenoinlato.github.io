Recital de Composição




entity

THSC / Composição

March 2023

Filmed at Teatro Helena Sá e Costa, Porto

Directing, camera, editing, audio mixing


I was invited by Gabriel Costa to do an audiovisual recording of a showcase featuring compositions from him and other fellow composers. Despite how close the invitation was to the showcase - less than a week away - I found it as a fun challenge to face. I couldn't do this alone though, as it would envolve several performers in totally different configurations and with different instruments, so I teamed up with Guilherme Marta, Sara Machado and Tomé Silva to make it work.

Despite some misteps in the recording process, as well as some limitations (non video friendly lights and limited recording angle), the result was pretty satisfactory, complemented by some surprisingly great contemporary compositions.


Direction: Leonardo Patrício
DOP, Technical: Leonardo Patrício
Micing: Sara Machado, Tomé Silva
Recording: Guilherme Marta
Audio Mixing: Leonardo Patrício
Video: Leonardo Patricio
