Projects

Pomares Music Fest

Founder / Creative Director

2023 - current

Culture is the art of the people. 2020 prefaced what was actually missing: space for people, for what makes them people. To think, live, coexist, see, listen, sweat - to move, deep down. Transfiguring the momento into yet another question, which aims to be elevated, complemented, undone or remade over and over again, which spreads across the dirt and fertilizes this living being that is Culture.

PMF (or Pomares Music Fest) is a small fest that happens every end of july at Pomares, Arganil, right at the north birthplace of the Azor mountains. It welcomes people from all around the country for performances from emerging artists and local community focused workshops and showcases.

@pomaresmusicfest

Fábio Borges

Co-producer / Technical Director

2023 - current

Fábio Borges brings new life to Coimbra's fado, a genre with a rich and wide history that has long been neglected. But more than recovering back lost and almost forgotten sounds, Fábio Borges is about reviving its connection with the people and sharing its collective soul.

@fadoborges
