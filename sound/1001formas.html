1001 Formas de Acordar



entity

Bernardo Chatillon / Coja-Animar

March 2024 - June 2024

Direction by Bernardo Chatillon

Performed by Coja-Animar

Music by Leonardo Patrício and Inês Luzio
Foley by Leonardo Patrício


This project appears as an invitation from Festival Trip, as part of the celebrations of the 50th anniversary of the 25th of April. What started as a game to explore each artist's individual investigations quickly evolved into a collective gesture, a manifesto. With a unique mix of theater and dance performance, ‘1001 Formas de Acordar’ is a piece where the youth expresses itself and recreates itself with the vitality that these celebrations deserve.
