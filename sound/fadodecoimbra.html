Fado de Coimbra

Fábio Borges

Released 19th october 2023

Recorded between february and july 2023

Produced by Fábio Borges

Recorded by Susana Brochado

Mixed and mastered by Leonardo Patrício

Fábio Borges's debut LP, culminated by many years of artistic grown in the midst of the fado scene in Coimbra, with a marked conviction in rejuvenating its root towards a more contemporary sound.
