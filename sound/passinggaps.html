PASSING GAPS





March - July 2023 -

A sonic installation by Leonardo Patrício



Space, the wrap, the medium through which either the outpouring or incarnation of the individual crosses, however limited to the construction of his reality, the elements he crosses and the trails he travesses– his real space. If he doesn't live by himself alone, maybe his shadow is found in the corridors, spaces of passage, reputed to the very punctual search function of reality, which all the other ditto seek, a set of realities external to the individual - a non-real space . What is certain is that all spaces are both filled and externalized with and by sounds, those that we try to stealthily reach like those that massage our passage. It is in a heterotopic relationship to the ambiguity between real and non-real space that this sound installation is fostered – transfiguring it into a liminal space, an encounter between the sound of the spatial reality of the individual who experiences it, with that of the reality of those who experienced it, a transitory condition between the known and the unknown, sailing through gaps between where we've been and where we're going.

Passing Gaps is a sonic experience, designed to be adapted to corridors and similar walking spaces, where the space interacts with a mysterious box called "non-real space". This interaction is mediated through a sound system and the individual's interaction with both spaces.

The video below is an audiovisual documentary around an inverse perspective on the installation itself. Whereas the installation pointed at sound from the perspective of space, the documentary pointed at the space from the perspective of the sound.
